My Projects

Recipe for Ruin

Recipe For Ruin is a roguelike where cooked food is the primary form of combat. This game is currently in development as part of a 20 week project for Junior Workshop with an 18 person team and is expected to be finished in March 2025. I am the lead mechanics developer responsible for creating implementations of the game's combat system.

Ultimate

Ultimate is a bullet hell style game that I developed as part of the GMAP 260 Overview of Computer Gaming class at Drexel. This game was developed over 5 weeks by a team of 5 of which I was the lead Unity developer. A web playable version of this game is playable here.

WacDonald's

WacDonald's is a top down 2D cooking game inspired by the gameplay of Overcooked and the spritework of the Pokemon franchise. This game was developed over 2 weeks by a team of 5 as part of Carnegie Mellon's National High School Game Academy program. I was responsible for designing and writing scripts in C# to implement the food cooking, bagging, and boost gameplay systems. After the program, my team and I decided to publish a version of this game to the MacOS App Store, available here. A web playable version is available here.
